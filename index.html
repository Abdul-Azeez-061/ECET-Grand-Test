How to Answer a Question

1
Click the option button to select your answer.
2
Click the same option again or Clear Response to deselect your chosen answer.
3
Click a different option to change your selection.
4
You MUST click Save & Next to record your answer — selecting alone is not sufficient.
5
To change a saved answer, navigate back to that question and repeat the selection procedure.
